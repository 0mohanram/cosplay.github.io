Cosplay???

Q&A

  • What is cosplay?
    • The term ‘Cosplay’ is a combination of the words “costume” and “play.” Cosplay is a performance art, a visual art, and a plastic art in which participants wear costumes and accessories to dress up like a character from anime, video game, and so on.
  • Why people love cosplay? What's the benefit behind cosplay?
    • People love to cosplay for many different reasons. Most of the cosplayers love cosplay because they learn many new skills such as make friends, how to sew, and build self-confidence, so on.
    • Nowadays, there are still lots of children, students, and adults that get bullied, but for some people, cosplay is a way to escape from reality.
    • Cosplayers won't judge you for the way you look or how much makeup you wear or even what you are wearing. Cosplay has taught people to make friends with others who are not typically from the same apple tree.
    • This is a very diverse social group, from age to ethnicity. While this hobby can be rather expensive, a lot of cosplayers create there own weapons and costumes to save money and further their expression of there personality.
    • This hobby has taught people to persevere and never give up, and the reason being is creating outfits and different characters within costumes. There can sometimes be roadblocks. Maybe something doesn't turn out the way they wanted it to, but this process encourages them to stay persistent.
    • When people are shy and locked away, cosplay is a great way to step out of the box and open your personality to the world. Personally, I used to be very shy. I was scared to start conversations with people at my school then when I found cosplay, it was like a whole new world; people are very polite and considerate. They ask permission to take pictures, and they are extremely friendly and easy to get to know.
  • What do I need to attend a exhibition?
    • You don't need lots of stuff to attends an exhibition. If you are attending a Comicon and have fun, you will only need tickets and your phone! If you are a coaplayer, you will need your wig, costume, accessories, and money to buy food, anime products. If you are a photographer, bring all of your camera accessories.
